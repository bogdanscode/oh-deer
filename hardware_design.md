# Design Template

## 1. Problem
My girlfriend's grandparents own a large backyard where they grow roses, snowball trees, and many other plants. As summer begins, deer are coming out to eat the plants. They visit mainly at night, when everyone is asleep, and they especially like to eat the roses.
The deer have eaten multiple roses and continue to come at night to eat more. My girlfriend's grandparents are considering using a "liquid fence," also known as coyote urine, to deter them.

## 2. Goal
The goal is to build a device that will scare away deer before they can eat the roses. The device should also look attractive so the grandparents will appreciate it in their garden.

## 3. Constraints
What limits do you have, such as budget, time, size, or safety?
Budget: $100
Time: 3 weeks
Size: no limit
Safety:
1. The device must not harm the deer
2. The device must not bother neighbors or grandparents
3. The device must look appealing to the grandparents so they will add it to their garden
